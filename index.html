ABOUT DJ TEASE

DJ Tease is a trailblazing DJ and performer known for his electrifying Amapiano and Afro-house sets that captivate audiences worldwide. With a signature look featuring a distinctive mask and traditional hat, he blends culture, mystery, and music to create an unforgettable stage presence. His YouTube channel has amassed over 11 million views, solidifying his reputation as a cutting-edge artist who fuses live instrumentation with high-energy mixing.

CAREER START & BACKGROUND

DJ Tease has been a force in the music industry for over a decade, carving out a unique space in the Amapiano and Afro-house scenes. His journey began in Cape Town, where his exceptional talent behind the decks earned him victory in a DJ competition, launching his career. From that moment, he dedicated himself to mastering his craft, refining his sound, and building a brand that fuses music, culture, and performance art. His signature look—featuring a mask and traditional hat—adds an element of intrigue and heritage to his performances, making him one of the most visually and sonically captivating DJs in the game.

ACHIEVEMENTS

DJ Tease has built an impressive career, earning recognition and a loyal following across multiple platforms. In 2023, he won the prestigious People's Choice Award, solidifying his status as a fan-favorite DJ. His talent has taken him beyond borders, with an international performance in Beira, Mozambique, as well as shows across South Africa in cities like Johannesburg, East London, and Pretoria.

BEYOND HIS MUSIC CAREER, DJ TEASE HAS BEEN A CAN DJ AMBASSADOR, ACTIVELY USING HIS PLATFORM TO SPREAD AWARENESS FOR CLIMATE ACTIONS NOW (CAN). HE HAS PERFORMED AT CAN EVENTS, CREATED ENGAGING CONTENT, AND COLLABORATED WITH OTHER ARTISTS TO PROMOTE SUSTAINABILITY THROUGH MUSIC.
