CAREER START & BACKGROUND

DJ Tease has been a force in the music industry for over a decade, carving out a unique space in the Amapiano and Afro-house scenes. His journey began in Cape Town, where his exceptional talent behind the decks earned him victory in a DJ competition, launching his career. From that moment, he dedicated himself to mastering his craft, refining his sound, and building a brand that fuses music, culture, and performance art.

ACHIEVEMENTS & UNIQUE STYLE

With a signature look featuring a distinctive mask and traditional hat, he blends culture, mystery, and music to create an unforgettable stage presence. His YouTube channel has amassed over 11 million views, solidifying his reputation as a cutting-edge artist who fuses live instrumentation with high-energy mixing. In 2023, he won the prestigious People's Choice Award, with performances across South Africa and internationally.
